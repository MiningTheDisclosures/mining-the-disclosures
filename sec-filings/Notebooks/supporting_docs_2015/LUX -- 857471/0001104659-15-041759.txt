<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-1.0in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Introduction</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Luxottica Group S.p.A. (the &#147;Group&#148; or &#147;Luxottica&#148;) is a leader in premium, luxury and sports eyewear with over 7,000 optical and sun retail stores in North America, Asia-Pacific, China, South Africa, Latin America and Europe and a strong, well-balanced brand portfolio. House brands include Ray-Ban, the world&#146;s most famous sun eyewear brand, Oakley, Vogue Eyewear, Persol, Oliver Peoples, Alain Mikli and Arnette, while licensed brands include Giorgio Armani, Bulgari, Burberry, Chanel, Coach, Dolce&nbsp;&amp; Gabbana, DKNY, Michael Kors, Polo Ralph Lauren, Prada, Starck Eyes, Tiffany and Versace. In addition to a global wholesale network covering more than 130 countries, the Group manages leading retail chains in major markets, including LensCrafters, Pearle Vision and ILORI in North America, OPSM and Laubman&nbsp;&amp; Pank in Asia-Pacific, LensCrafters in China, GMO in Latin America and Sunglass Hut worldwide. The Group&#146;s products are designed and manufactured at its six manufacturing plants in Italy, three wholly owned plants in the People&#146;s Republic of China, one plant in Brazil, one small plant in India and one plant in the United States devoted to the production of high performance and prescription eyewear.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Luxottica has manufactured and contracted to manufacture products as to which certain Designated Minerals (as defined below) are necessary to the functionality or production of such products.&#160; After exercising reasonable due diligence as required by Rule&nbsp;13p-1 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, Luxottica was unable to determine whether the tin, tantalum, tungsten and/or gold (&#147;Designated Minerals&#148;) used in one or more of its products where such Designated Minerals are necessary to such product(s) functionality or production originated from the Democratic Republic of the Congo or an adjoining country that shares an internationally recognized border with the Democratic Republic of the Congo.</font></p>

<div style="font-family:Times New Roman;">
<p align="right" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:right;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Exhibit&nbsp;1.01</font></b></p>
<p align="right" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:right;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Luxottica Group S.p.A.</font></b></p>
<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Conflict Minerals Report</font></b></p>
<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">For The Year Ended December&nbsp;31, 2014</font></b></p>
<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">This Conflict Minerals Report for the year ended December&nbsp;31, 2014 is presented to comply with Rule&nbsp;13p-1 under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (the &#147;Rule&#148;).&#160; The Rule&nbsp;was adopted by the Securities and Exchange Commission (&#147;SEC&#148;) to implement reporting and disclosure requirements related to conflict minerals as directed by the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act of 2010.&#160; The Company&#146;s due diligence process, described below, is based on the methodology and policies in accordance with <i>Annex 1 &#151; Five Step Framework for Risk-Based Due Diligence in the Mineral Supply Chain from the Organization for Economic Co-Operation and Development (&#147;</i>OECD<i>&#148;) Due Diligence Guidance for Responsible Supply Chains of Minerals from Conflict-Affected and High-Risk Areas</i> (the &#147;OECD Framework&#148;).&#160; The list of products covered by this Conflict Minerals Report is included in Attachment A hereto, which is an integral part of this Conflicts Minerals Report.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">DUE DILIGENCE PROCEDURES</font></p>
<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">1.</font><font size="1" style="font-size:8.5pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><u><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Establish strong company management systems.</font></u></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">We have adopted and communicated to our supply chain vendors Luxottica&#146;s&#160; policy governing the supply chain of minerals originating from conflict-affected or high-risk areas, which provides that we:</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 56.4pt;text-indent:-.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(i)</font><font size="1" style="font-size:8.5pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">expect our supply chain vendors to source materials from socially responsible suppliers, including legitimate conflict-free mines;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 56.4pt;text-indent:-.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(ii)</font><font size="1" style="font-size:8.5pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">will work with supply chain vendors to ensure proper and timely disclosure of the potential use of conflict minerals that could be introduced into our supply chain; and</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 56.4pt;text-indent:-.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(iii)</font><font size="1" style="font-size:8.5pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">will require our supply chain vendors to conduct the necessary due diligence and provide us with proper verification of the source of the materials used in products that are intended to be incorporated into or sold as a Luxottica product.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 56.4pt;text-indent:-.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">A conflict minerals reporting system has been established to ensure our supply chain vendors are aware of Luxottica&#146;s policy to continually monitor Luxottica&#146;s suppliers and to implement an obligation to report potential conflict mineral-related risks or issues to Company management.&#160; These requirements and obligations are carried out by Luxottica&#146;s &#147;Responsible Sourcing&#148; team and are included in the team&#146;s procedures.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">2.</font><font size="1" style="font-size:8.5pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><u><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Steps taken to identify risks in the supply chain</font></u><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">For calendar year 2014, Luxottica followed an approach for risk-based due diligence in accordance with the OECD Framework.&#160; Specifically, Luxottica followed the process set forth below to determine the use, source and origin of conflict minerals, if any, in its global product portfolio across Luxottica&#146;s businesses:</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:8.5pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Prepared an inventory of our manufacturing processes, including materials used in our products, whether manufactured by us or by a contracted third party.&#160; When we determined that conflict minerals may be used in our products, an inventory was prepared of all materials used in the relevant manufacturing process.&#160; Luxottica&#146;s products are manufactured primarily at our facilities in Italy, China, the United States, Brazil, and India and by third parties in Europe, China, Japan, France and South Korea.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:8.5pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">In cases where we identified that a &#147;conflict mineral&#148; is included in the product (e.g., gold and tin), we evaluated whether that mineral is &#147;necessary for the functionality or production&#148; of the product.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<div style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><hr size="3" width="100%" noshade color="#010101" align="left"></div>
</div>

<br clear="all" style="page-break-before:always;">
<div style="font-family:Times New Roman;">
<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">3.</font><font size="1" style="font-size:8.5pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><u><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Design and implement a strategy to respond to identified risks.</font></u></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">In situations where it was determined that a &#147;conflict mineral&#148; is necessary for the functionality or production of at least one of our products, we carried out a reasonable country of origin inquiry (&#147;RCOI&#148;).</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:8.5pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Utilizing the Conflict-Free Sourcing Initiative Conflict Minerals Reporting Template (&#147;CMRT template&#148; or &#147;survey&#148;) for direct Luxottica vendors and indirect vendors, as necessary.&#160; Additionally, in a separate communication, Luxottica alerted potential &#147;conflict minerals&#148; vendors of its conflict minerals policy and the expectation that our suppliers source materials only from &#147;socially responsible&#148; suppliers, including legitimate conflict-free mines in the Democratic Republic of the Congo region.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Luxottica also documented its due diligence process, as suggested by the OECD guidelines, which includes:</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:8.5pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Conducting site visits as part of our responsible sourcing activities and in-person interviews with vendors within our supply chain as part of our ongoing due diligence protocol, as deemed necessary based on our risk assessment and responses to the survey.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:8.5pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">When determined to be necessary, confirmation that the vendor utilizes a smelter in the Conflict-Free Smelter (&#147;CFS&#148;) program, if known, or that such minerals were derived from recycled or scrap sources.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Our continuous application of systems, procedures and strong governance processes is designed to ensure proper implementation of Luxottica&#146;s responsible sourcing strategy, which includes conflict minerals and was applied throughout Luxottica&#146;s vertically integrated business. These systems, procedures and processes may change from time to time due to enhancements adopted and implemented by Luxottica.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">4.</font><font size="1" style="font-size:8.5pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><u><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Carry out independent third-party audits</font></u><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Luxottica has partnered with others in community and industry organizations to understand if and when an independent third-party audit of a supplier may be necessary.&#160; We will rely on a supplier&#146;s audit in concluding that a supplier is utilizing Conflict-Free Sourcing in its supply chain.&#160; In addition, we will also utilize tools available through the Electronic Industry Citizenship Coalition (&#147;EICC&#148;) and the Global e-Sustainability Initiative (&#147;GeSI&#148;) which are sponsors of the Conflict-Free Sourcing Initiative (&#147;CFSI&#148;) supporting the sourcing of conflict-free minerals and will rely on audits performed under CFSI protocols. The CFSI is a global leader for the responsible sourcing of minerals. It provides information on conflict-free smelters and refiners, common tools to gather sourcing information and forums for exchanging best practices on addressing conflicts minerals.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">5.</font><font size="1" style="font-size:8.5pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><u><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Supply chain due diligence reporting</font></u><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">A reporting system is being developed to disclose risks and issues regarding our &#147;Responsible Sourcing&#148; policy to senior management, which will cover conflict mineral-related risks or issues. In addition, a link to Luxottica&#146;s Conflict Minerals Statement can be found at: &nbsp;<u>www.luxottica.com/en/company/our-way/our-way-doing-business/conflict-minerals-sourcing</u>.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">6.</font><font size="1" style="font-size:8.5pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><u><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Results for the 2014 calendar year</font></u><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">As of December&nbsp;31, 2014, we have applied our due diligence process to a significant portion of our supply chain and our manufacturing and production processes. For calendar year 2014, we identified Luxottica products that contained, or may contain, conflict minerals and the vendors supplying such conflict minerals to our supply chain.&#160; Currently, we have not been alerted to the use of conflict minerals originating from the DRC region that would necessitate a determination of whether they were sourced from a legitimate, conflict-free mine.</font></p>
