Item 1.01     Conflict Minerals Disclosure and Report

BorgWarner Inc. ("BorgWarner" or the "Company"), after performing the steps required by Rule 13p-1 did not determine whether tin, tantalum, tungsten and/or gold ("3TG") used in one or more of its products originated from the Democratic Republic of the Congo ("DRC") or an adjoining country that shares an internationally recognized border with the DRC (the "Conflict Region"). BorgWarner has no reason to believe that 3TG in products manufactured by BorgWarner may have originated in the Conflict Region.
